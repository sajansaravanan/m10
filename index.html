NASA's NGC 4254 Spiral Galaxy

The spiral galaxy NGC 4254 showcases dazzling swirls and intricate structures.

Located approximately 55 million light-years away in the constellation Virgo, it is part of the Virgo Cluster.

NGC 4254 is characterized by its prominent spiral arms and bright center, indicating ongoing star formation.

Observations from NASA's telescopes provide insights into the dynamics of spiral galaxies.

Studying galaxies like NGC 4254 helps scientists understand the evolution and structure of the universe.
